Government announces major digital transformation plan for 2026.

By ASHTVBD News Desk Â· Published: Today Â· Dhaka

The government has unveiled an ambitious digital transformation roadmap aimed at expanding online services, improving connectivity, and supporting innovation across key sectors including education, healthcare, and public administration.

Officials say the initiative will focus on building modern infrastructure, enhancing cybersecurity, and promoting digital literacy nationwide. The plan includes new investments in broadband networks, cloud platforms, and AI-driven public services.

Key goals of the initiative

  • Expanding high-speed internet access to rural and remote areas.
  • Introducing digital ID and secure online verification systems.
  • Modernizing public service delivery through automation.
  • Supporting startups and tech innovation hubs.

Experts believe the transformation could significantly boost economic growth and create new opportunities for young professionals in the technology sector.

More details are expected to be released in the coming weeks as the government finalizes the implementation timeline and budget allocations.
